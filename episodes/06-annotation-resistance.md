## Introduction

Use *cyp51A* as the main example. The commonly discussed environmental azole-resistance mechanisms include promoter tandem-repeat plus coding-change combinations such as TR34/L98H and TR46/Y121F/T289A. These mechanisms are widely reported in azole-resistant *A. fumigatus* and can be found in patients without previous azole exposure. 

**Databases**
Mention MARDy and FungAMR as resources for known fungal resistance mechanisms. MARDy is described as a curated database of known resistance mechanisms and associated antifungal drugs. FungAMR was published in 2025 as a manually curated fungal AMR mutation resource covering many drugs, genes, and fungal species.

::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::: keypoints

- Annotation adds biological context to variants. 
- Known resistance mutations support prediction, not certainty. 
- Absence of a known mutation does not prove susceptibility.
